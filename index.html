Adult Fundamentals

Join us Monday, Wednesday, and Friday night at 6:30PM to learn the fundamentals of the sport. In this class students have the opportunity to learn Jiu Jitsu in a clean, friendly, low-pressure environment. Students learn from black belt instructors through drills, live rolling, and one-on-one training. Take your confidence to a new level in a fun, dynamic way. Contact us today to learn more about each of our course offerings.

View Schedule

Adult Advanced

Take your Jiu Jitsu to the next level. In this class we train many techniques, focusing on the key aspects of form and technique. You will learn a wide array of skills, from advanced basics to competition-style techniques. Individuals who have attained the rank of white belt with 2 stripes and above are welcome to attend this class.

View Schedule

Ladies-only

Ladies looking for a safe, female-only environment to learn Jiu Jitsu can take advantage of this class. You will learn the fundamentals of Jiu Jitsu from our black belt instructor with other women in this collaborative, self defense focused class.

View Schedule

Kid's Fundamentals

Ages 7-12:
This course teaches your kids all of the skills they need to get into BJJ. We'll help them gain confidence in life and sport. Some benefits for your child learning Brazillian Jiu Jitsu include:

  • Increased discipline
  • Increased focus
  • Improved balance & coordination
  • Increased confidence
  • Leg-up against other beginners when they graduate to the fundamentals course

Expert Coaches

Our Jiu Jitsu coaches are affiliated with Alliance Brazillian Jiu Jitsu. Both the Owner/Head Coach August Gabiola & our Program Director Westen Waltjen are first degree black belts in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. They are passionate about the sport, and about helping their students excel in Jiu Jitsu.

Owner / Head Coach

August Gabiola

Owner, Operator, and head coach. August is a 1st Degree Black Belt in B.J.J., active CrossFit practitioner, and all-around pressure pass monster. August recieved his black belt from Mestre Gigi Paiva, and enjoys seeing his students see the benefit of hard work.

Head Coach / Program Director

Westen Waltjen

Our Program Director Westen is a 1st Degree Black Belt in B.J.J. who recieved his black belt from B.J. Penn's gym. He pushes his students to excellence and emphasizes technique over strength-only B.J.J. He also enjoys snowboarding and the outdoors.

Assistant Coach

Keone Valdez

Keone is a purple belt in B.J.J. who joined SIBJJ a few months after its inception. He has been training for 9 years. He has a passion for instilling excellence in all his students, and a desire to rise above mediocrity in the sport. Keone enjoys promoting the sport, sleight-of-hand, and motivating older & newer students to keep training.

Assistant Coach

Nick Jones

Nick is a purple belt in B.J.J. who competes in both gi and nogi tournaments. He has developed a nogi style that takes advantage of flexibility and technique over brute strength. He enjoys training people and watching them accomplish their goals. His leg lock game is one of the best in the gym.
